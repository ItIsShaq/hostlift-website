About HostLift

We're Former Superhosts Who Cracked the Algorithm

Born from frustration, built for hosts.

HostLift was founded in 2024 by a team of multi-property Superhosts who were obsessed with understanding why some listings dominated while identical properties next door sat empty. After years of testing, reverse-engineering Airbnb's search algorithm, and tracking guest behavior across thousands of bookings, we built the methodology that now powers our agency.

Today, our team of 14 optimization specialists, copywriters, and data analysts serve hosts from Miami to Milan helping them earn significantly more without cutting nightly rates or investing in expensive renovations.
